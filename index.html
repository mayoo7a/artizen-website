Private Events

Celebrate your occasion at Artisan with a private art session in a relaxed, creative environment. Host a gathering, pick the activity that suits your group, and make art together — birthdays, bridal showers, team days, or just a night with your people.

  • Themed table arrangement in a color of your choice — flowers, candles, covers & cake disposables (3 JDs setup per person)
  • You're welcome to bring your own food
  • All bookings include access to the coffee corner
